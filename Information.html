Information About Us

Petals is a business founded by six year 11 girls from marist college. We started petals because we believe that hygiene is very important especially for teenagers. We wanted to give everyone in marist college access to high quality aesthetic soaps.This is why we use a shea butter base as it is very moisturizing and we try to have our prices as affordable as we can. Petals soaps come in three different variations such as; a cloud soap that is blue with camomile flowers and is scented with grapefruit and camomile essential oils, A heart shaped soap coloured purple and is scented with lemongrass and lavender essential oils with lavender flowers. Lastly we have mini honeycombs that will have no color and will have daisy flowers with lemon essential oils and natural honey.
